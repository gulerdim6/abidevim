About Hekate

HEKATE (Hecate) was the goddess of magic, witchcraft, the night, moon, ghosts and necromancy.Hekate assisted Demeter in her search for Persephone, guiding her through the night with flaming torches. After the mother-daughter reunion became she Persephone's minister and companion in Haides.

Hekate helps Persephone

Hekate’s characteristics

  1. She’s the black she-dog
  2. She’s polecat (a mustelid house pet kept by the ancients to hunt vermin).
  3. She returns with lampades and ghosts.
  4. She’s worker from a far.
  5. She’s Persephone's minister
